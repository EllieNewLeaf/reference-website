Who is Neytirix?

Warning! Her work may not be for the feint of heart since she does a lot of gory/scary content as well, I have put only PG friendly images on this webpage from her work

Neytirix is an Australian musician and artist. She is known for her equally comedic and dark content, and the way they contrast each other. Her content often contains happy themes with extremely edgy visuals, or vice-versa, which has earned her a unique style of content. Almost half on her YouTube videos are speedpaint videos, often of a more dark look on a usually happy and jolly thing (take, for example, the tellytubbies video). The rest are simple art or music-oriented videos, with short jokes and sketches in her style.

Neytirix is also known for her "Character Redesign" series, in which she draws other people's OCs and often turns them into horrific versions of themselves as well as the way she advertises her sponsor Skillshare at the start of her videos - instead of just talking about it, she creates a situation for her characters to introduce and promote Skillshare to the viewers.
